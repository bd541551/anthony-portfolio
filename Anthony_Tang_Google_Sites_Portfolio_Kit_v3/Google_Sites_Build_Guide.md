## Project 1 — WIP Engagement Forecasting

**Business problem**  
Leadership needed a more reliable way to compare current-year and prior-year engagement activity and estimate future work-in-progress hours.

**Approach**  
- Engineered nested historical comparison models across Azure, Databricks, and SQL Server
- Prepared reusable modeling datasets and feature pipelines
- Deployed Ridge Regression with scikit-learn
- Designed the workflow for use by non-technical stakeholders

**Result**  
Achieved an R² of approximately 0.88 and improved visibility into resource-planning requirements.

**Tools**  
Python · scikit-learn · SQL · Azure · Databricks

---

## Project 2 — Enterprise Reporting & KPI Governance

**Business problem**  
Cross-functional teams needed consistent definitions, reliable metrics, and self-service access to financial and operational reporting.

**Approach**  
- Designed and maintained more than 30 Power BI dashboards
- Built advanced DAX and semantic data models
- Integrated Azure KQL, SQL, and Qualtrics data
- Established KPI definitions and reporting governance standards

**Result**  
Supported daily decision-making and contributed to a 10% improvement in budget allocation accuracy.

**Tools**  
Power BI · DAX · KQL · SQL · Python · Data Governance

---

## Project 3 — Cross-Border Transaction Analytics

**Business problem**  
Physical commodity transactions required coordinated commercial analysis across supplier prices, product grades, logistics costs, and international execution.

**Approach**  
- Evaluated grade mix and board-foot volume
- Modeled supplier, fumigation, rail, ocean, and freight costs
- Coordinated suppliers, freight forwarders, and overseas buyers
- Managed invoices, bills of lading, amendments, container planning, and discrepancy resolution

**Result**  
Supported more than $2M in hardwood transactions connecting U.S. suppliers with buyers in China, Singapore, and Vietnam.

**Capabilities**  
Commercial Analytics · Negotiation · Logistics · Cross-Cultural Communication

A global supply-chain team needed better visibility into upstream suppliers and sourcing risk.

**Tools**
